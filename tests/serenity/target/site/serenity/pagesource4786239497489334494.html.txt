    <div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-9 col-md-9 col-lg-9 comntent">
        <h2>The Future of Apache Storm w/ Taylor Goetz</h2>
        <p>At over 3 years old as an open source project, and an Apache project for more than 2 years, Apache Storm is one of the most mature and widely adopted real-time data platforms available.
